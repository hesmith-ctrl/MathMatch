MathMatch

A Henry Smith Production

How to Play

Enter Your Names: Start by entering player names, or keep the defaults.

Understand Your Equation: Each player is given a unique equation with a mix of numbers and the variable "x" on both sides.

Draw a Number: At each turn, a number is drawn for each player.

Apply an Operation: Each player will apply a mathematical operation (+, -, ×, ÷) to their equation by replacing the drawn number into their equation.

Next Turn: After applying your operation, press "Next Turn" to proceed to the next round and draw new numbers.

Winning the Game: The first player to simplify their equation to have only "x" on one side is the winner!
